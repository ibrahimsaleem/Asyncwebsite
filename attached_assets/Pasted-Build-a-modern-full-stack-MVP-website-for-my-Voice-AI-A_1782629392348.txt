Build a modern full-stack MVP website for my Voice AI Agent business called **Aisync**.

Use the attached presentation as the main source for business details, branding, copy, services, and value proposition.

Business summary:
Aisync provides a human-like AI voice calling agent for businesses. The AI agent answers inbound calls, makes outbound follow-ups, books appointments, answers FAQs, updates CRM/calendar systems, sends reminders, and works 24/7 so businesses never miss another call.

Main message:
**Never miss another call.**
A human-like AI voice agent that answers, books, and follows up for your business every hour of every day.

Target customers:
Business owners, operations leaders, clinics, restaurants, real estate teams, service businesses, and any business that depends on phone calls.

Keep the app simple, premium, clean, and professional. Do not overbuild. First create a working MVP with a beautiful landing page, client portal, and admin dashboard.

Tech stack:
Use a simple Replit-friendly full-stack setup:

* React or Next.js frontend
* Node.js/Express backend if needed
* Database using PostgreSQL, SQLite, or Replit DB
* Authentication
* Role-based access control
* File upload support
* Invoice/payment tracking

Design style:

* Premium SaaS look
* Dark/navy/black and white theme with modern accent colors
* Clean cards, rounded corners, smooth spacing
* Professional landing page like a modern AI startup
* Mobile responsive
* Simple animations only if easy
* Use placeholder data where needed
